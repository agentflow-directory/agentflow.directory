# Local Crisis Survival Planner

A no-code emergency preparedness agent that generates **clear, printable, location-aware survival checklists**.

---

## What this agent does

Helps users prepare for:

- Power blackouts
- Infrastructure disruptions
- Shelter-in-place situations
- Emerging risks (including drone-related awareness scenarios)

---

## Outputs

- Water supply plan
- Food checklist
- Medicine kit
- Equipment list
- Action plan (first 24–72 hours)
- Local government resources

---

## Example prompts

- "3-day blackout survival plan for 2 people in Virginia"
- "7-day emergency checklist for a family of 4"
- "Printable emergency preparedness checklist"
- "What to do in the first 6 hours of a blackout?"

---

## Setup

1. Create Custom GPT
2. Paste system prompt
3. Upload knowledge files (optional)
